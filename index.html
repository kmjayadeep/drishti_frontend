What is DRIS HTI

We are on the edge of another technological revolution brought about by development in different fields like Artificial intelligence , Virtual reality , Augmented reality, IoT and on. And just like the information revolution changed our life this is going to redefine the way we live and work.
We believe that understanding these technological change enables a larger community of people to live a better life. We see technology as the greatest enabler of society. As one of the prestigious technology institution of the state we see it as our responsibility to impart knowledge of these technologies to the entire community inside and outside college. Drsihti is our tool through which we give life to this mission.
